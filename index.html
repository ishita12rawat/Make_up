Eyeliner: is a popular cosmetic product used to define and accentuate the eyes. It comes in various forms and colors and can be applied in different styles to achieve various looks. Here’s an overview of different types of eyeliners, their uses, and application tips Mascaras Purpose: Darkens, lengthens, and volumizes eyelashes. Types: Volumizing, lengthening, and waterproof. Application: Applied to upper and lower lashes with a wand. 7. Eyeshadows Purpose: Adds color and dimension to the eyelids. Types: Powder, cream, pencil, and liquid. Application: Applied to eyelids with brushes or applicators. 8. Lipsticks Purpose: Adds color and texture to the lips. Types: Matte, glossy, satin, and liquid. Application: Applied directly from the bullet or with a brush. 9. Lip Glosses Purpose: Provides shine and a hint of color to the lips. Types: Clear, tinted, and glitter. Application: Applied directly to the lips. 10. Highlighters Purpose: Adds a luminous glow to specific areas of the face. Types: Powder, cream, liquid, and stick. Application: Applied to high points of the face like cheekbones and brow bones. 11. Contour Products Purpose: Defines and shapes the face by creating shadows and highlights. Types: Powder, cream, and stick. Application: Applied to areas of the face where shadows naturally occur (like under cheekbones). 12. Setting Sprays Purpose: Locks makeup in place and controls shine. Types: Hydrating, mattifying, and long-lasting. Application: Sprayed over the face after makeup application. 13. Makeup Removers Purpose: Removes makeup and cleanses the skin. Types: Cleansing wipes, micellar water, and oil-based removers. Application: Applied with cotton pads or directly to the skin.
